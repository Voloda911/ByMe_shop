byMe x Steel Wings Foundation

The byMe x Steel Wings Foundation collaboration is a charity project: We donate 100% of the proceeds from the sale of the items to purchase drones. In cooperation with the international charity Steel Wings Foundation, we created a capsule collection of items to raise money for FPV drones for the 110th Separate Mechanised Brigade named after Major General Mark Bezruchko.

Everyone's contribution is crucial. Our position is to emphasise the importance of everyone being involved and aware of the need for our help to defend Ukraine's defenders. This is a mandatory contribution from everyone behind the front line. The byMe clothing brand covered all the costs of creating the items. Buying an item from this collection means contributing to our common struggle.

ukrainian clothing brand uMe: WE WORK TO WIN

Ukrainian clothing brand byMe has always been and will always be about important meanings. Despite the fact that this is not a usual seasonal or capsule collection, we have chosen special details for the clothes. We want every thing told the world about our strength and encouraged us to act without ceasing - because that's the only way we can to win and defend what is ours. "Steel Wings" and byMe are a combination of the driving force and the value of meaning. That's why you will find designs with wings on the clothes, which have become a symbol of the joint collection: it includes warm clothes and meaningful accessories.

in colobration made both warm women's clothing and warm men's clothing.

Fleece sweatshirts for men. These are warm long sleeves made of soft fabric. They have a high collar with a short zip, and a place for a chevron on the sleeve - they are also special in the collection. You can buy fleece sweatshirts with chevrons stylised for the collaboration chevrons. Fleece hoodies for women. The women's hoodies are fluffy and milky in colour. They have a hood and a slightly lowered seam line on the shoulders. Fleeces will keep you warm in autumn and winter.
