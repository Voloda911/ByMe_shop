The men's collection is particularly important to us. Until recently, we only produced women's clothing and are now focused on it. But the men's clothing from this collection is our first and confident step to make not only Ukrainian women fall in love with Ukrainian, but also Ukrainian men. Ume clothing manufacturer is all about things that matter. Men's clothing embodies this meaning with its comfort and authenticity.

Branded menswear that makes a difference matter

Menswear by bvMe is a capsule collection of clothes that can be used to create a complete look. It will be united not only by colours or style, but also by an idea. The main idea is comfortable and functional clothing. We'll tell you about its features later, but for now, take a look at our branded menswear:

  • t-shirts
  • sweatshirts
  • trousers
  • sheletka

What makes them special?

  • Attention to detail. All the products have functional large and small pockets, reflective elements, drawstrings - they are important both as decorative elements and as practical ones to store small items or adjust the size, etc.
  • Classic colours. All things are combined with each other primarily by colours: black, white, cream - this adds to the look of restraint and lightness. Such items will also be a good base for outfits with accent shoes or accessories.
  • Loose fit. Convenience comes first, so all items are loose-fitting: they won't interfere with your movements. And oversize fans will be able to choose an item one size up to make it even more comfortable.
  • Branding. The menswear belongs to the My Language is My Armour collection. This collection is about the power of each of us to fight and defend with the Ukrainian word. We depicted this reminder on the items in the collection. But its special feature is the chevrons. We left places on the clothes where you can attach chevrons. Or use the "My language is my armour" chevron for your military uniform.

Our goal is to dispel the stereotype that buying men's clothing means choosing the first thing you pick up. Because things have meaning. We give them this meaning and then broadcast it to the world. ByMe clothes convey the indomitable spirit and courage to be Free.

You can buy men's clothing right here on the website or by visiting the showroom in Kyiv. The byMe brand is a space for free, ideological and inspired people. Join us!
